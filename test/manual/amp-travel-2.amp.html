Edmonton in Winter

Edmonton is the capital of Alberta, Canada. Edmonton is on the North Saskatchewan River and is the centre of the Edmonton Capital Region, which is surrounded by Alberta's central region.

Ice Castles at Hawrelak Park

For the first time in forever, there is a glistening acre-sized castle made of ice in Hawrelak Park! It's brought to us by the folks at Silver Skate Festival (Feb 12-21) and it's a castle you'll want to visit many times over the winter as it changes shape.

Elk Island National Park

Elk Island National Park falls within the Beaver Hills Dark Sky Preserve, which means optimal conditions for seeking out sparkling star clusters, nebulae and galaxies, and even catching a glimpse of the bright, pulsating chorus of lights we know as the Northern Lights.

Snowshoeing in Edmonton

So what if it’s the middle of winter? You’re ready to explore! Strap on a pair of snowshoes and you’re off on an exhilarating day trip through Edmonton’s 7,400 hectares of parkland and 150 kilometres of interconnected trails.
